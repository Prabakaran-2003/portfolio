About

I’m Prabakaran S, a Biomedical Engineering graduate from Kongunadu College of Engineering and Technology, Tamil Nadu. I’ve worked on projects like a Heart Attack Detection System, IoT-based Paralysis Care, and Wearable Bio-Monitoring Systems focused on improving patient care. Internships at MEDVU, Retna Global, and Kabila Hospitals gave me practical biomedical experience. I’m certified in Arduino programming, medical coding, and electronics design. Passionate about biomedical instrumentation and pytotherapy tech, I combine empathy with strong communication and a constant eagerness to learn, aiming to develop innovative healthcare solutions that enhance lives.
