About

I am Prabakaran S, a Biomedical Engineering graduate from Kongunadu College of Engineering and Technology. My hands-on experience through hospital internships and certifications in Arduino, biomedical hardware, and medical coding has strengthened my technical expertise. My goal is to contribute to innovative healthcare solutions that enhance patient care and medical efficiency through technology-driven research and development.
