About

Prabakaran S holds a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Biomedical Engineering from Kongunadu College of Engineering and Technology, Thottiyam, Tamil Nadu. With a strong academic foundation and practical exposure in biomedical systems, he is passionate about applying engineering principles to drive innovation in healthcare. His academic projects include the development of a Heart Attack Detection System, a Paralysis Care System, and a Wearable Bio-Monitoring System—each aimed at enhancing clinical diagnostics and patient monitoring. He has gained valuable hands-on experience through internships at MEDVU, Retna Global Health Hospital, and Kabila Hospital, where he worked closely with hospital-grade biomedical equipment and healthcare technologies. Beyond academics, Prabakaran has actively participated in seminars, paper presentations, and technical workshops. He is certified in Arduino programming, electronic system design, and medical coding. His key interests lie in biomedical instrumentation and physiotherapy technologies. Known for his compassion, strong communication skills, and eagerness to learn, Prabakaran is committed to advancing medical technology through interdisciplinary collaboration. His goal is to contribute innovative solutions that improve patient care and healthcare efficiency.
